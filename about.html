About Me

My Story

Hi, I’m Mike - UI Designer by profession, moonlight photographer, NBA basketball player in my dreams!

Welcome to my site. Drawing was most definitely my first creative love. It was clear from an early age I was fascinated with and passionate about all things visual. With a natural curiosity and a mind for math, the relationship between art and science for me has always been an intimate one. Schooled at Sheridan college in drawing, painting, classical animation and Film&TV; specializing in VFX & video editing, I have a unique ability to apply design principles across many forms of media.

Having worked as marketing manager and senior graphic designer in the high pace real estate industry for many years I’m able to deliver high quality results while multi-tasking on tight time lines. This experience combined with years of freelance graphic design and photography have helped me cultivate a powerful combination of creative vision, interpersonal skills, sensitivity to user needs and technical ability.

Continuing my education with career Foundry I’ve gained essential skills in UI/UX. Fueled with a passion for visual storytelling and an endless desire to capture the captivating. Other interests include video editing and motion graphics.
